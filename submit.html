HevrutAI accepts submissions of contested claims, AI-generated distortions, historical inversions, and narrative manipulations for human-supervised review. High-quality submissions that pass review become permanent documented cases in the Opposition Library.

How submissions work
01 — SUBMIT You describe the claim, where you found it, and what makes it worth investigating.
02 — RESEARCH Your submission enters an automated research pipeline that traces origins, spread vectors, and historical precedent.
03 — REVIEW A human reviewer evaluates the research and determines whether the case warrants full archive status.
04 — ARCHIVE Verified cases enter the Opposition Library as permanent, citable public records.
